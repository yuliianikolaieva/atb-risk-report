ATB Additional Agreement — Risk Assessment

Review of the proposed Additional Agreement to the services/works contract with LLC «ATB-MARKET» (Customer), where Bolt acts as Contractor. This briefing highlights the clauses flagged by the auditors, describes the risk, its likelihood and its potential financial impact, with each clause shown in Ukrainian and English.

Counterparty: ТОВ «АТБ-МАРКЕТ» Document: Додаткова угода (Additional Agreement) Auditors: UHY · Baker Tilly Prepared: June 2026

Executive summary

The Additional Agreement shifts almost all tax, criminal-procedure and registration risk onto Bolt as Contractor, with broad, one-sided liability and several penalties for events Bolt does not fully control.

What management needs to decide

  • Unlimited, one-sided liability. Bolt would compensate the Customer for tax losses, penalties and even budget recovery — in some cases up to 100% of the contract revenue — frequently regardless of proven fault.
  • Liability for events outside Bolt's control (blocked/suspended tax-invoice registration, third-party fictitious counterparties, criminal proceedings without a verdict).
  • Cash-flow exposure. 20% of every payment is deferred until tax-invoice registration, and the Customer may freeze payments at its discretion.
  • Operational burden & fixed penalties (UAH 10k / 20k / 100k) for administrative breaches and shortened registration deadlines.
  • Key mitigant (Baker Tilly): the contractual transfer of tax / public-law liability is likely legally unenforceable — per the Supreme Court ruling of 6 Feb 2026 (case No. 904/2953/24), such provisions "do not give rise to the legal consequences they are intended to produce". Several penalties are assessed as punitive and inconsistent with this position.
  • Recommendation: negotiate caps on liability, a fault requirement, removal of liability for state-side / counterparty actions outside Bolt's control, and realistic deadlines — see auditor comments per clause below.

Key financial risks

Clauses with the largest potential financial exposure for Bolt.

Clause 2 Contractor assumes all negative consequences & potential risks Critical
LikelihoodMedium
ImpactCritical
OverallCritical
Українською

«Виконавець бере на себе відповідальність за всі негативні наслідки та потенційні ризики, що будь-яким чином пов’язані, або можуть бути пов’язані із фінансово-господарськими відносинами між Замовником та Виконавцем за даним Договором.»

English

"The Contractor assumes responsibility for all negative consequences and potential risks that are in any way related, or may be related, to the financial and business relations between the Customer and the Contractor under this Agreement."

Risk: Unlimited, one-sided allocation of tax and business liability to Bolt — an open-ended "catch-all" obligation.

Potential impact
  • Unlimited financial exposure for Bolt, with no liability cap.
UHY comment

Unlimited allocation of tax liability to Bolt (one-sided risk allocation) → unlimited financial exposure for Bolt.

Baker Tilly comment

Risk & assessment: Bolt becomes a quasi-guarantor of the counterparty's VAT/CIT exposure for any tax irregularities. This contradicts the Supreme Court ruling of 6 Feb 2026 (case No. 904/2953/24): contractual transfer of public-law / tax liability is invalid and produces no legal consequences. Likelihood: Medium–High (e.g. an unregistered VAT invoice affecting ATB's input VAT would likely become litigious).

Clause 2.1 Compensation of taxes, penalties, lost VAT credit & budget recovery Critical
LikelihoodMedium
ImpactCritical
OverallCritical
Українською

Нарахування контролюючими органами податків, зборів, штрафних санкцій; зменшення податкового кредиту Замовника з ПДВ за податковими накладними Виконавця; та/або збільшення об’єкта оподаткування з податку на прибуток; або стягнення в дохід держави отриманого за угодою, визнаною недійсною.

English

Imposition by tax authorities of taxes, fees and penalties; reduction of the Customer's VAT input tax credit based on the Contractor's tax invoices; and/or increase of the Customer's taxable profit base; or a court decision ordering recovery to the state budget of amounts received under an agreement declared invalid.

Risk: Compensation of all tax penalties even in the absence of proven fault by Bolt.

Potential impact
  • Lost VAT input tax credit — 20% of service value + penalties / late-payment interest (additional up to ~25%).
  • Increase of corporate income tax base — 18% of service value.
  • Recovery to the state budget — potentially 100% of Bolt's revenue under this Agreement.
UHY comment

The Customer acknowledges that tax-authority decisions may be deemed unlawful and cancelled; in that case reimbursement to the Contractor may apply, but only after the funds are returned by the tax authorities to the Customer.

Baker Tilly comment

It compels payment before the final determination of the tax position and includes legal fees with no reasonableness standard or threshold — effectively converting public-law uncertainty into an immediate private-law debt.

Відкриття кримінального провадження, де у будь-якому процесуальному статусі (або без такого) фігурує Виконавець або будь-яка з його посадових осіб (директор, бухгалтер) — за переліком статей Кримінального кодексу.

English

Negative consequences include the initiation of criminal proceedings in which the Contractor or any of its officers (director / general director, accountant / chief accountant) appears in any procedural status (or without any status).

Risk: Consequences (payment suspension, liability) arise even in the absence of a court verdict.

UHY comment

Consequences even in the absence of a court verdict — reputational and operational risk.

Baker Tilly comment

Risk: Adverse contractual consequences may arise merely upon initiation of criminal proceedings involving Bolt or its officers, irrespective of outcome — exposure to payment suspension without anyone being criminally liable. Likelihood: Medium–High — proceedings in Ukraine may remain open for long periods once initiated.

Clause 2.2 / 2.3 Suspension of payments by the Customer Medium–High
LikelihoodHigh
ImpactMedium
OverallMedium–High
Українською

Строк оплати відтерміновується до моменту остаточного усунення негативних наслідків та потенційних ризиків; таке відтермінування не вважається порушенням зобов’язань Замовника з оплати.

English

The payment term is deferred until the negative consequences / potential risks are fully eliminated; such deferral is deemed not to be a breach of the Customer's payment obligation.

Risk: The Customer effectively freezes payments at its discretion, with no late-payment liability.

Potential impact
  • Cash-flow risk — payments withheld for an indefinite period.
UHY comment

Suspension of payments — the Customer effectively freezes payments → cash-flow risk.

Baker Tilly comment

Risk: ATB may suspend payments on broadly and subjectively defined grounds, for an indefinite period controlled by third parties (tax office, courts), despite proper performance by Bolt. Likelihood: High — the suspension threshold is broad and subjective.

У разі зупинки/відмови Замовнику в реєстрації розрахунку коригування (обов’язок реєстрації якого покладено на Замовника) внаслідок зупинки реєстрації податкової накладної — Виконавець сплачує штраф у розмірі ПДВ, але не менше 1 000 грн за кожне порушення.

Compensation of VAT for blocked tax invoices, despite the Contractor having no actual control over the suspension of registration.
